About Coverings

A covering refers to the breeding of a mare and a stallion. You can choose to accept a public or private covering offer for your mare.

Accepting a covering offer allows you to strategically enhance a bloodline by producing foals with superior genetics.

There are three different types of coverings: public, private, and team.

Public Covering
Posted by a player on the public coverings page, where it becomes available for any player to accept for their mare.
Private Covering
Posted between two specific players in a reserved exchange or is posted by you to use for one of your own mares.
Team Covering
Posted by players who are on the same team in a reserved exchange to use only on mares who hold the team affix. These coverings are only given out by stallions with the team affix as well.
